# Competitor Landscape — Math Mistake Diagnosis

**Date:** 2026-07-22 · **Context:** competitive analysis for Proposal A, Error Archaeologist (錯誤考古學家) — diagnoses *why* a student made a math mistake, not just marks it wrong.

Market splits into three tiers. Our wedge sits between Tier 1 (solvers, no *why*) and Tier 2 (learn/understand, but locked or hint-only).

## Tier 1 — Solver / Checker (consumer, weakest at "why")

| Product | Product range | Pain point addressed | Pricing |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Photomath | Scan → step-by-step solution, mobile | "I'm stuck, show me steps" | Free tier; Plus **$9.99/mo** or **$69.99/yr** |
| Gauth (ByteDance) | Photo homework solver, 24/7 | Instant answer, any subject | Free tier; **~$9.99/mo** |
| Mathos / Symbolab / Wolfram Alpha | Solver + calculator | Compute + check work | Free → ~$6–20/mo |

Gap they leave open: they solve the *correct* path; they do NOT diagnose the student's *wrong* path. This is our wedge.

## Tier 2 — Learn / Understand (closest philosophical neighbors)

| Product | Product range | Pain point addressed | Pricing |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Khanmigo (Khan Academy) | Socratic AI tutor, withholds answer | "Understand, don't cheat" | **~$4/mo** / **$44/yr** per learner |
| MATHia (Carnegie Learning) | Adaptive coaching G6–12; recognizes *why* students err, routes to missing prerequisite | Misconception → prerequisite gap | Enterprise/district license (not public, per-seat district deal) |
| Class Companion | Student practice + AI feedback + teacher misconception dashboard | Teacher sees class-wide misconceptions | Free teacher tier; paid school/district |

**MATHia = real competitor** — closest to "why they make specific mistakes." But walled district product, adaptive-curriculum shaped, not a fast standalone diagnostic. **Class Companion owns the teacher-dashboard angle.**

## Tier 3 — Teacher grading tools (adjacent)

CoGrader, Graded Pro, Kuraplan — grade handwritten work, flag misconceptions, LMS integration. **$10–30/mo** individual, district tiers above. Grading-first, diagnosis-second.

## Pricing models — three patterns

1. **Consumer freemium** — free + **$4–10/mo** (Khanmigo, Photomath, Gauth). Volume play.
2. **Teacher-free / district-paid** — free individual teacher, seat license for school/district (Class Companion, CoGrader). Land-and-expand.
3. **Enterprise-only** — no public price, district contract (MATHia).

## Where our wedge sits

No consumer-facing product does **evidence-linked *why*-diagnosis + deterministic verification + honest abstention**. Photomath/Gauth solve; Khanmigo Socratic-hints; MATHia diagnoses but locked in district curriculum. Differentiator vs all: **verifiable diagnosis of the specific error + refuses to guess when unclear** — solver tier can't, MATHia doesn't ship standalone.

Pricing fit if judges ask: position teacher-free / district-paid (Tier 2 pattern) — matches Education track, avoids consumer race-to-$4. Do not commit a price in the demo.
